Private hotel «Mogul» is located in the beautiful historical area in the center of Vorokhta (30-40 people occupancy, 20km from the skiing resort «Bukovel», transportation to the skiing slopes are fast and confortable). It is interesting here during winter and summer.
Important advantage of the location is presence of developed touristic and resting infrastructures, that is adapted to any season, as well as close location of the mountain Hoverla for summer hiking. Staying in the comfortable wooden one or two bedroom houses with all facilities. Easy parking. Every room has cabel TV , WI-FI, electric kettle, fridge. The bulding has laundry room, caffee, ski rental. You can find train station, bank, ATM, shopping outlet, sauna, "Stara Vorokhta" restaurant nearby.
You can order breakfast, lunch or dinner in the hotel, rent skiing equipment or bikes. You can get skiing instructor, guide, taxi.
